# Bitkey brand study

## Initial observations from https://bitkey.world/

### Overall visual language
The homepage uses a **dark charcoal / blue-black canvas** with very high-contrast **white display typography**. The visual tone is industrial, secure, and product-led rather than decorative. The hero relies on dramatic scale, strong negative space, and product renders placed around oversized messaging.

### Typography
- **Body font:** `haffer, "haffer Fallback"`
- **Hero display font:** `foundersGroteskCondensed, "foundersGroteskCondensed Fallback"`
- Hero headline is **uppercase**, extremely large, condensed, and bold.
- Supporting text and buttons use a more neutral sans-serif with a technical, modern feel.

### Hero composition
- Top-left: simple Bitkey wordmark.
- Top-right: a light pill CTA plus compact circular utility icons.
- Main hero: floating hardware render near the upper-left, giant stacked headline centered, small uppercase sub-line beneath, primary and secondary pill CTAs below.
- Lower hero area includes a rotated phone/app mockup and partner brand line (“From the makers of Square + Cash App”).

### Buttons
- Primary CTA appears as a **rounded pill** with a muted **rose / mauve accent** fill and dark text.
- Secondary CTA is a **dark pill with light border/text**.
- Buttons are uppercase and compact, with strong visual weight.

### Components and sections
- Cards and sections feel roomy, modular, and dark-themed.
- Rounded corners are used, but not overly soft.
- The site emphasizes product trust, recovery, and self-custody with editorial spacing.

### Footer structure
- Footer appears link-dense, organized into navigation and support areas.
- Social links and legal links are included.
- Country selector is integrated near the footer area.

### Known computed style facts captured from the page
- `body` font family: `haffer, "haffer Fallback"`
- `h1` font family: `foundersGroteskCondensed, "foundersGroteskCondensed Fallback"`

## Lower-page and footer observations

The lower content area shifts to a **light editorial grid** with three article cards arranged as broad vertical columns. Each card uses a restrained monochrome treatment, generous spacing, and compact uppercase eyebrow labels above larger black headlines. This creates a stark contrast against the dark hero and reinforces the brand’s balance between technical confidence and editorial calm.

The footer returns to a **dark textured background**, likely a stone or cosmic mineral surface, with a very large white **Bitkey** wordmark anchored across the lower-left area. Link groups are organized in narrow columns with small uppercase headings such as navigation, learning, support, and connect. A compact white QR-style download card sits to the lower-right, and the partner attribution for **Square** and **Cash App** appears near the oversized footer wordmark. The footer therefore feels both branded and utility-focused rather than minimalist.

Across the sampled areas, the design language suggests three important structural principles: strong contrast between dark product-led spaces and light editorial spaces, oversized brand typography used as a compositional device, and pill-shaped interactive controls paired with strict uppercase labeling.
